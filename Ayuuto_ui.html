About Ayuuto

Community-based savings platform inspired by traditional Somali rotation savings. Ayuuto is a system in which a small group of people agree to contribute a fixed amount of money on a set schedule. During each cycle, one member receives the full pooled payout. The goal of Ayuuto is to take a system that is usually tracked informally—through memory, paper, or group chats—and make it transparent, organized, and fair by digitally managing members, contribution history, payout order, reminders, and group rules. Instead of replacing trust, Ayuuto supports it by giving everyone a clear view of who has paid, who is next to receive funds, and how the group is progressing over time.
We located in Saint Paul, Minnesota.
